About The Readers Hut

Our Story

Welcome to The Readers Hut, your premier destination for honest book reviews, literary discussions, and a vibrant reading community. Founded by Nwaeke Chinaza Fortune, a passionate Nigerian pharmacist and entrepreneur based in Port Harcourt, our journey began in 2018 with a simple mission: to make quality books more accessible to Nigerian readers.

What started as a small community of book lovers on Instagram has evolved into a thriving online bookstore and reading platform that connects thousands of readers across Nigeria and beyond. We believe that every book has a story to tell, and every reader deserves access to those stories.

What We Do

Honest Reviews

Authentic, relatable book reviews that help you discover your next favorite read.

Reading Community

A vibrant book club where readers connect, discuss, and share their love for literature.

Book Accessibility

Making quality books accessible to Nigerian readers through our online bookstore.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We provide genuine, heartfelt reviews without pretense
  • Community: Building connections among book lovers across borders
  • Accessibility: Making literature available to everyone
  • Diversity: Celebrating books from all cultures and perspectives

Meet the Founder

Nwaeke Chinaza Fortune is a Nigerian pharmacist, entrepreneur, and passionate reader who transformed her love for books into a thriving community. Despite the challenges of book pricing and logistics in Nigeria, Chinaza has successfully grown The Readers Hut from a simple Instagram page into a comprehensive platform serving thousands of book lovers.

When not reviewing books or running the bookstore, you'll find Chinaza sharing her thoughts on literature, contributing to platforms like BellaNaija, and building connections within the Nigerian literary community.
